Welcome to What I hope will be a enlightening and knowledge filled experience

This app will take a look and both Mental Health And Sexual Health, detailing the affects of each, The supports your there for each, and helping the user find their local supports and providing well needed information, and shaking away any misconceptions about each. Below there are 3 buttons each bring you to different hubs. The Map hub is the location based map that will bring you to the supports you are looking for. The Sexual Health hub is a informative experience using information gathered from reliable sources and aimed and given you, the user a understanding of everything to do with sexual health and how different things can affect you. The Mental Health hub is in a similar vein to the Sexual Health hub as it has information gathered from reliable sources helping you understand the subject matter better and making you aware of the supports around you
